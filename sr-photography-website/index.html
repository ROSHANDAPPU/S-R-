PROJECT TIMELINE

01
Initial Consultation
We start with a friendly consultation to understand your vision, requirements, and expectations. This helps us capture your story the way you imagine it.
02
Customized Proposal
Based on your needs, we prepare a tailored package with clear pricing, deliverables, and timelines. No hidden costs, everything transparent.
03
Booking & Agreement
Once you are ready to move forward, we secure your date with a simple contract and deposit. This ensures your special day is locked in.
04
Pre-Shoot Planning
We work closely with you to finalize shoot details such as location, outfits, themes, and shot list. For events, we also plan the timeline to make sure nothing is missed.
05
The Shoot
On the big day, our team arrives on time, fully equipped, and focused on capturing every detail. We blend creative direction with candid moments to give you the perfect mix.
06
Delivery & Memories
After editing and retouching, your final photos and videos are delivered in beautiful high-resolution format, ready to share and cherish forever.
